A little bit about me.

My name is Gretta, I'm a full-time graphic designer working just southwest of Minneapolis. Aside from graphic design, I spend many nights working on digital illustrations and am currently delving into front-end development (that's right, I hand-coded this very site!). Outside of my beloved technology, I consider myself a nail-art enthusiest and a seasonal knitter.

As a designer, I consider myself an optimistic realist. I believe in enthralling plans with plausible endings. I have a great appreciation and understanding of the rules which enables me to break them with precision. As an illustrator, I find my style is either extremely sharp or wonderfully imperfect. Professionally I have gravitated towards avatar drawing, from family portraits to entire departments.

I'm always on my email.
